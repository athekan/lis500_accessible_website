Social Activites

Breakout Groups

In-person attendees:

Please listen to the instructions provided by the presenter to locate your breakout groups for discussion. In these breakout groups, we expect all attendees to follow our inclusion statement below.

Online attendees:

Please navigate to the breakout rooms that are assigned to you on Zoom. A pop-up should appear on the meeting page that allows you to change rooms to your breakout room. If one does not appear, please contact us as soon as possible.

Q&A Session

In-person attendees:

Please raise your hand if you are able, and a staff member will come to you with a microphone. Input to the workshop leaders is suggested, but not required, so feel free to just listen along as you desire. In Q&A sessions, we expect all attendees to follow our inclusion statement below.

Online attendees:

Please use the “raise hand” button on the Zoom menu bar during these Q&A sessions to designate a question to the presenter. During other sessions, please do not use this function, as it could interrupt the live presentation for other viewers.

Experiments

Experiment sessions are focused on strengthening your learning through demonstration and experimentation. Failure is a normal and necessary part of learning how to code, so do not lose hope if you run into trouble.

In-person attendees:

If assistance is needed, please flag a designated staff member down to assist you.

Online attendees:

If assistance is needed, please use the “raise hand” button on the Zoom menu bar and a staff member will invite you to a private rom where you can ask questions.

Inclusion statement

At our website-building conference, we believe that everyone should feel welcome and included. We are committed to creating a safe and accessible environment where attendees of all backgrounds and abilities can come together to learn and grow.

  • Accessibility: We strive to make our event accessible to everyone, regardless of their abilities. We offer a variety of accommodations to ensure that all attendees can participate fully in the event. This includes providing sign language interpreters, closed captioning, and accessible seating. If you require any additional accommodations to attend the event, please contact us and we will do our best to meet your needs.
  • Diversity: We believe that diversity is essential to creating a vibrant and inclusive community. We welcome attendees from all backgrounds, cultures, and perspectives, and we are committed to fostering an environment where everyone feels valued and respected. We encourage attendees to share their experiences and perspectives with each other to create a truly inclusive and enriching learning experience.
  • Community: We are dedicated to building a supportive and welcoming community around our event. We believe that everyone has something to contribute and that we are all stronger when we work together. We encourage attendees to connect with each other, share their knowledge and expertise, and build lasting relationships that extend beyond the event.

We are committed to creating an inclusive and welcoming environment at our website-building conference. If you have any questions or concerns about accessibility or inclusion, please don't hesitate to contact us. We look forward to welcoming you to our event!

Safety Statement

The safety and well-being of our attendees is our top priority. We are committed to creating a safe and secure environment where everyone can learn and network without fear of harm or harassment.


COVID-19 Safety Measures:

  • Requiring masks to be worn at all times indoors
  • Providing hand sanitizer and encouraging regular hand-washing
  • Regularly cleaning and disinfecting all high-touch surfaces
  • Screening attendees for symptoms of COVID-19 before allowing them to enter the event

We ask that all attendees comply with these safety measures to help protect themselves and others.
